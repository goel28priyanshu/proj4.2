GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Read these instructions carefully before taking the quiz test.
  2. Total duration of examination or quiz is 30 minutes.
  3. The countdown timer in the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You will not be required to end or submit your examination.
  4. You may submit your paper after 15 minutes of quiz starting(or Submit button activates after 15 minutes) and before it you are not required to leave.
  5. The quiz contains 4 sections:
    • SUB1
    • SUB2
    • SUB3
    • SUB4
  6. Each section contains 5 questions.
  7. For a correct answer you will be awarded with +4 marks,and 1 mark is deducted for each wrong answer.
  8. Unattempted questions gave you 0 mark.
  9. A navigation bar is available at the quiz where all the sections are shown to you and by clicking any one of them redirect you to respective section.
  10. The examinee is required to bring its own pen and paper.
  11. Use of mobile phones or cell phones or digital watches or calculators is strictly prohibited.
  12. You are regularly under observation.
  13. To select your answer, click on one of the options for a given question.
  14. To change your answer, click on other option of the question.
  15. Result is declared after the examination or quiz completion.

    16. IMPORTANT:After clicking START QUIZ button (given below) your test or quiz starts.
